SFSU CIDER LAB
Research Overview

Cybersecurity and Intelligent Distributed Edge Research (CIDER) Lab focuses on advancing next-generation intelligent wireless communication, edge intelligence applications, and affordable AI solutions. Our work emphasizes efficiency and security, leveraging federated learning, generative AI, GNNs, and LLMs to address challenges in communication, security, and intelligent applications while ensuring scalability, privacy, and adaptability.

  • Federated Learning with MPC
  • Module-Based CNN for IRS-Assisted Spectrum Sensing
  • Generative AI–Based IoT Attack Detection
  • GNN-Based Physical Layer Security with Power Allocation
  • Vision Camera–Based Hand Tracking & Gesture Recognition
  • Phishing URL and Phishing UI Detection
  • On-Device LLM–Based Recycle Bin Design
  • LLM-Based 3D Layout Design
